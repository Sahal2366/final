Ustadh Sahal

Ustadh Sahal started memorizing the Quran at the age of 12 years old. The Ustadh learned with the esteemed Qari Abdelkarim Edgouch and finished the ENTIRE Quran in less than a year at the age of 13 years old. Later on, Ustadh Sahal got certified in 'riwayat hafs' and tajweed, memorizing both Matn Jazariyah and Tuhfatul Atfaal with Sheikh Muhammad Mabrook from Al-Azhar University.

Not only is he the Imam at Farouq Masjid located in Grand Prairie, Texas, he is also the YOUNGEST Imam in the Dallas Fort Worth area at the age of 21. Recently our Ustadh received an acceptance from the esteemd University of Madinah, and will further his Islamic Knowledge in Sharia (Islamic Law).
